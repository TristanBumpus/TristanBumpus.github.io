Scrap Planet

A roguelike about exploring world of scrap. Using a magnet to deal with hords of enemies in the long twisted hallways of a lab.

Art made by HooperGooper

Devlog

Scrap Planet was made in about 72 hours for the melon jam 2024. It got 86 place overall out of 231 entries, but it got 25 for innovation. In this game I tried to make some custome physics for the magnets and the object interactions, I also did a loot tables for the first time and a unique camera setup.
