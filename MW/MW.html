Mecha War

Your'e going to war on an alien planet, but the aliens are fighting back. Destroy the city and survive. Collect their parts to fix your self and lets see how long you can survive.

Art by Pepper Studio

Devlog

This was a game made in about a week as a personnal jam between me and a friend. The game was in preperation for a competition coming up in ValleyField. The game was made using Godot and all assets were made in Maya and Asprite. With every game I try to learn something new and with this one I published my first 3D game, I worked on optimizations and 3D pathfinding.
