Felonious

Explore a land full of merchants to plunder and marines to run from. All to destroy the King that broke his promise.

Sound and Music by HooperGlooper Art by Pepper Studio

Devlog

This was a game made in 48 hours for the ValleyField Intercollegial game Jam 2026. The theme was vessel and the team decided to make a parite looting game. In this game I made cutscenes and changeable keybindings for the first time. Overall we where able to keep the game in a small enough scoop and the development went smoothly.
